# Privacy Policy for Hermes WHOOP Integration

Last updated: May 23, 2026

This application is a personal, private integration used only by its owner to

retrieve health and fitness data from WHOOP and make it available to a locally

hosted Hermes Agent.

## Data Accessed

The application may access the following WHOOP data, depending on the permissions granted:

- Recovery data, including recovery score, HRV, resting heart rate, SpO2, and skin temperature

- Sleep data

- Cycle and strain data

- Workout data, if enabled

## Purpose of Processing

The data is retrieved solely for personal health tracking, summaries, trend analysis,

and contextual use by the owner's locally hosted Hermes Agent.

## Data Storage

Data and authentication tokens are stored locally on the owner's Raspberry Pi.

The application does not operate a public service and does not provide access to other users.

## Data Sharing

The application does not sell, rent, or share WHOOP data with third parties.

Data may be processed by the locally configured Hermes Agent and its explicitly

configured memory provider, if enabled by the owner.

## Data Retention and Deletion

The owner controls all stored data locally and may delete stored WHOOP data,

tokens, or Hermes memory entries at any time.

## Access Revocation

Access to WHOOP data can be revoked by removing the application's authorization

through WHOOP or by deleting the locally stored authentication tokens.
